    Concept Releases

    Here you will find all concepts that were introduced during the development of the ECLS CDM and are included in the OMOP Extension vocabulary. These concepts serve as high-level references when using the OHDSI toolchain (e.g., Atlas) for data analysis, as the ECLS CDM tables — like other extension tables — are not directly accessible through these tools.
